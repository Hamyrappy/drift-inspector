Drift Inspector

Measuring and exploring scientific drift with Atomic Contribution Claims

An LLM distills every abstract into atomic contribution claims — what a paper actually adds, stripped of motivation and boilerplate. Claims are embedded, clustered across years, and served as an interactive map where every field-level trend can be traced down to the individual claims and papers behind it.
